Covid Updates

One can get the updates regarding covid statistics of a place in this section of our website. This section of the website has a wealth of information, including daily confirmed cases, daily deaths, daily recovered patients, the overall number of confirmed cases, and the total number of fatalities in a country, state, or district. With the assistance of a graph, this information is shown on the screen. This page has a unique feature in that it displays all of the aforementioned information as well as the geographical location of the desired site on a map.

To utilise this feature, the user must first log in.
The Donor section of the Covid Survivors website is one of the most essential areas.
People who need blood are known as blood receivers. They must choose their blood group from the alternatives offered, and they will be sent to a list of hospitals that have blood available from both covid recovered patients and healthy persons.
Blood recipients can use the contact information given to contact the appropriate hospital based on their needs.
Blood donors may search for local hospitals and make an appointment to donate blood. After booking the appointment, he/she must check the profile page for confirmation.
